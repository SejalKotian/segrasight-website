The Problem

Hospitals generate enormous volumes of biomedical waste every day — from contaminated plastics and sharps to infectious materials. A single large hospital can produce several tons of waste daily, and correct segregation is required at the point of disposal.

In practice, segregation is manual, time-constrained, and error-prone. Misclassification can contaminate recycling streams, increase treatment costs (e.g., treating general waste as regulated medical waste), and create serious safety risks for healthcare and sanitation workers.

Improper disposal can also trigger audits, operational disruptions, and regulatory penalties that can reach thousands of dollars per violation depending on jurisdiction and severity — plus reputational risk.

Our Solution

SegraSight builds intelligent waste terminals powered by AI and hyperspectral imaging to enable accurate, error-free biomedical waste disposal in hospital environments.

By identifying waste materials in real time and guiding correct disposal, SegraSight reduces misclassification, improves compliance, and lowers operational and regulatory costs for healthcare facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become the recognized leader in intelligent medical waste segregation by delivering automated, compliant, and sustainable disposal solutions tailored to the needs of modern healthcare facilities.
