The Problem

Hospitals generate enormous volumes of biomedical waste every day — from contaminated plastics and sharps to infectious materials. A single large hospital can produce several tons of waste daily, much of which must be segregated correctly at the point of disposal.

In practice, waste segregation is manual, error-prone, and time-constrained. Studies estimate that a significant fraction of hospital waste is misclassified, leading to contaminated recycling streams, increased treatment costs, and serious safety risks for healthcare and sanitation workers.

Regulatory penalties for improper biomedical waste disposal can reach thousands of dollars per violation, in addition to long-term compliance risks, audits, and reputational damage for healthcare facilities.

Our Solution

SegraSight builds intelligent waste terminals powered by AI and hyperspectral imaging to enable accurate, error-free biomedical waste disposal in hospital environments.

By identifying waste materials in real time and guiding correct disposal, SegraSight reduces misclassification, improves compliance, and lowers operational and regulatory costs for healthcare facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become the recognized leader in intelligent medical waste segregation by delivering automated, compliant, and sustainable disposal solutions tailored to the needs of modern healthcare facilities.
